About Me

Beyond technology, I am also passionate about fashion design and UI/UX design. Eager to contribute to the tech community, I am passionate about sharing my expertise and inspiring others to pursue their tech dreams. My key skills include proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, experience with various frontend development frameworks, strong problem-solving and analytical skills, effective communication and collaboration skills, and fashion design and UI/UX design skills.

With a diverse range of interests and expertise, I am currently enrolled in a rigorous bootcamp with BGIT, honing my skills as a frontend developer while expanding my knowledge base.

Passion for Fashion and Technology:

With a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of user experience, I am a passionate fashion UI/UX designer who is driven by the intersection of creativity and technology. My two-year internship at a private company as a QA engineer and automation engineer provided me with valuable insights into the technical aspects of software development, while my ongoing participation in the BGIT bootcamp for frontend development is further enhancing my skills in creating visually appealing and user-friendly interfaces.

Combining Creativity and Technical Expertise:

I am particularly fascinated by the challenge of translating the essence of fashion brands into engaging digital experiences. Through my UI/UX design work, I strive to capture the unique brand identity and convey it seamlessly across various digital platforms, from e-commerce websites to mobile apps and social media presences. My ability to balance creativity with technical expertise enables me to craft designs that are both aesthetically pleasing and functionally sound.

Enthusiastic Learner and Collaborative Spirit:

I am an enthusiastic learner who is always eager to expand my knowledge and skills in the ever-evolving field of fashion UI/UX design. I am also a strong collaborator, finding joy in working closely with designers, developers, and marketing teams to create a cohesive and impactful digital presence for fashion brands. My passion for fashion and my commitment to user-centered design make me a valuable asset to any team seeking to create a truly exceptional fashion experience.
